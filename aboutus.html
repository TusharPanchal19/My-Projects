About Us

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate and dedicated students pursuing our Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

This Blood Donation Management System is a project developed as part of our academic journey, aiming to solve real-world problems through technology. Our goal is to build a bridge between blood donors and those in need, ensuring quick and efficient blood availability during emergencies.

As AI & ML enthusiasts, we plan to enhance this platform in the future with smart features such as intelligent donor matching, data analytics, and predictive health assistance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make blood donation more accessible, organized, and technology-driven. We believe that by creating a user-friendly platform, we can encourage more people to donate blood and save lives.
